Project Overview

Designed and developed a user-centric COVID-19 tracking app using Dart and Flutter. Overcoming challenges with data accuracy and real-time updates, I created a visually appealing and interactive platform offering:

  • Combated misinformation in the pandemic: Developed a Covid-19 tracking app using Dart and Flutter, providing users with reliable global/local case updates and curated news sources to counter misinformation. (Project Link)
  • Empowered proactive health management: Built self-assessment quizzes within the app, enabling users to monitor potential symptoms and make informed decisions about their health.
  • Fostered community awareness: Disseminated real-time Covid-19 data and relevant news within the app, keeping users informed and promoting community-wide vigilance.
